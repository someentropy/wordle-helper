How to Play Wordle

Wordle is a simple but addictive word game where you try to guess a hidden 5-letter word in six attempts or less. After each guess, the game gives you feedback using colors:

W
O
R
D
S
  • W – Correct letter, correct position (green)
  • R – Letter is in the word, but in the wrong position (yellow)
  • O, D, S – Letters not in the word at all (gray)

Tips for Playing

  • Start with a word that uses common letters. Our top-ranked starting word is ALERT, based on letter frequency across all official Wordle answers.
  • Use the color feedback to narrow down possibilities.
  • Green letters should stay in place, yellow letters should be moved, and gray letters should be avoided.
  • Use a solver tool (like ours!) if you're stuck or want help.
