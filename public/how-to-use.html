How to Use the Wordle Solver

This guide will help you get the best results using the Wordle Solver:

  1. 🟩 Green letters: Enter letters that are correct and in the correct position.
  2. 🟨 Yellow letters: Enter letters that are correct but in the wrong position. Click the ❌ buttons to mark positions they should not be in.
  3. ⬛ Excluded letters: Add letters you know are not in the word at all.
  4. 🎯 Click Search to get ranked word suggestions based on your clues.
  5. 🧹 Use Clear to reset all clues and start fresh.

Have fun and solve smarter!
